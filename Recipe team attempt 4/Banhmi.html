Delicious Banh Mi at Home!

The perfect fusion of French and Vietnamese cuisine, for sandwich and culture lovers!

Did you know...

Banh Mi originated in Vietnam during the French colonial period, combining French and Vietnamese ingredients.

In a Pinch, or if you are me. The easiest way to prepare the chicken (in general, but especially here) is in your air fryer. Coat the chicken with your marinade of choice and let it sit for a few minutes while your air fryer preheats, and then depending on your cut(and type of chicken), air fry it for 16-23 minutes at 380

Ingredients

  • Baguette 1 large baguette
  • Pork belly or chicken 200g of pork or chicken
  • Pickled vegetables Carrot and daikon, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro A handful of fresh cilantro
  • Sriracha To taste (optional)
  • Mayonnaise 2 tbsp mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill or pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the pork or chicken until fully cooked (about 5-7 minutes per side).
  3. While the protein cooks, slice the baguette and spread mayonnaise on the inside.
  4. Slice the grilled pork or chicken thinly and place it inside the baguette.
  5. Add pickled carrots, daikon, cilantro, and a dash of soy sauce to the sandwich.
  6. Drizzle sriracha or add chili peppers for spice, if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious fusion of French and Vietnamese flavors!
